Example data is a 2D-matrix where the rows contain the observations at each time sample from each electrode at the resting state where subject is instructed to keep eyes open or close.
Data contains the recordings on each of the 16 EEG electrodes and was bandpass filtered to alpha (8-12Hz).  

The locations of the electrodes were FP1, FP2, FC5, FC6, FZ, T7, CZ, T8, P7, P3, PZ, P4, P8, O1, Oz, and O2. 
The reference was placed on the right earlobe and the ground at the AFZ scalp location.

sample rate: 512 Hz
time length: 10 seconds
